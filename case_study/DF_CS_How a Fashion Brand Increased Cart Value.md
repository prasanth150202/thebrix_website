# How a Fashion Brand Increased Cart Value with Complete-Look Recommendations Using BRIX

In fashion ecommerce, customers often visit a store with a specific purchase in mind. They may add a kurta, a T-shirt, or a pair of jeans to their cart and proceed directly to checkout. While the sale is successful, the brand misses an opportunity to recommend complementary products that complete the outfit. Every missed recommendation represents lost revenue and a lower Average Order Value (AOV).

This case study explores how a fashion brand solved this challenge using **BRIX Complete-Look Recommendations**. Instead of showing random product suggestions, BRIX displayed carefully curated outfit combinations that matched the customer's selected item. Whether it was pairing a kurta with bottom wear and a dupatta or recommending joggers and accessories alongside a T-shirt, the strategy helped customers discover products that naturally belonged together. The result was higher cross-sell engagement, improved product attach rates, stronger AOV, and significant additional revenue from complete-look purchases.

## Why Complete-Look Recommendations Matter

Fashion shopping is highly visual and style-driven. Customers don't just buy clothing-they buy complete outfits. However, when an online store only highlights individual products, shoppers may never discover matching items that complement their purchase.

Complete-look recommendations bridge this gap by making styling effortless. Instead of asking customers to search for coordinating pieces, the store presents them at the right moment during the shopping journey. This improves the customer experience while increasing the likelihood of additional purchases.

For fashion brands, this strategy delivers two major benefits. Customers enjoy a convenient shopping experience with curated outfit suggestions, while merchants increase Average Order Value without spending more on customer acquisition.

## The Challenge

### Customers Purchased Only One Product

The fashion brand generated consistent traffic through paid advertising and organic marketing. Although conversions were healthy, most customers purchased only one item per order. A shopper buying a kurta rarely explored matching pants or a coordinating dupatta. Similarly, customers purchasing T-shirts often overlooked joggers, caps, or accessories that complemented their style.

The issue wasn't product quality or pricing-it was product discovery. Customers simply weren't being introduced to matching products at the right stage of their shopping journey.

### Lost Cross-Selling Opportunities

Every single-product order represented missed revenue. Since complementary products weren't showcased effectively, customers completed their purchases without realizing the brand offered matching styles.

The business recognized that encouraging shoppers to build complete outfits would be more profitable than increasing advertising spend. They needed a solution that felt helpful rather than promotional.

## The BRIX Solution

### Smart Complete-Look Recommendations

The brand implemented **BRIX Complete-Look Recommendations**, allowing shoppers to discover complementary products directly on product pages and inside the cart.

Instead of generic "You may also like" sections, BRIX displayed curated outfit combinations based on the selected product.

### **Example Outfit Combinations**

| **Primary Product** | **Recommended Products** |
| ------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Kurta               | Bottom Wear + Dupatta    |
| T-Shirt             | Joggers + Accessories    |
| Shirt               | Chinos + Belt            |
| Dress               | Handbag + Footwear       |

These recommendations helped customers visualize an entire outfit instead of a single product.

### AI-Powered Cross-Sell Suggestions

BRIX intelligently recommended products based on category relationships, purchase history, and merchandising rules. Every recommendation was relevant to the shopper's intent, increasing the likelihood of additional purchases.

Rather than overwhelming customers with dozens of products, BRIX showcased only the most suitable complementary items, creating a cleaner and more personalized shopping experience.

### Personalized Fashion Combos

The platform also enabled merchants to create pre-configured style combinations for seasonal collections, festive wear, office attire, and casual outfits.

Customers could complete their entire look with just a few clicks, eliminating the need to browse multiple collections.

### Implementation Strategy

The fashion brand began by identifying products frequently purchased together using previous sales data. Merchandising teams then created complete-look combinations that reflected current fashion trends and customer preferences.

Recommendations were strategically placed on product pages, cart drawers, and mini carts to ensure maximum visibility without interrupting the shopping experience.

BRIX also prioritized complementary products with similar colors, patterns, and styles, helping customers build cohesive outfits instead of receiving unrelated suggestions. This relevance significantly improved engagement and cross-sell performance.

## Results After Implementing BRIX

Within weeks of launching Complete-Look Recommendations, the brand recorded measurable improvements across several ecommerce metrics.

| **Metric**            | **Before BRIX** | **After BRIX** |
| --------------------- | --------------- | -------------- |
| Cross-Sell Click Rate | 9%              | 27%            |
| Product Attach Rate   | 16%             | 38%            |
| Average Order Value   | ₹1,480          | ₹1,860 (+26%)  |

### Higher Cross-Sell Clicks

Relevant outfit suggestions captured customer attention immediately. Cross-sell click rates increased from **9% to 27%**, demonstrating that shoppers were actively exploring complete-look recommendations.

### Improved Product Attach Rate

The percentage of customers purchasing multiple products in a single order increased significantly. The product attach rate grew from **16% to 38%**, indicating stronger engagement with complementary recommendations.

### Growth in Average Order Value

By encouraging shoppers to complete their outfits, the fashion brand achieved a **26% increase in Average Order Value** without offering additional discounts or increasing advertising budgets.

### Increased Combo Revenue

Complete-look purchases became a valuable revenue stream, generating approximately **₹5.4 lakhs in additional monthly revenue** through cross-selling alone.

## Why Complete-Look Recommendations Work

Fashion customers often seek inspiration as much as they seek products. When a store presents a thoughtfully curated outfit, it removes uncertainty and simplifies the buying decision. Instead of wondering what pairs well with a kurta or T-shirt, customers receive instant styling suggestions that feel personalized and practical.

Complete-look recommendations also reduce the effort required to discover matching products. By presenting complementary items in the same shopping session, BRIX increases convenience while naturally encouraging larger purchases.

This strategy benefits both shoppers and merchants. Customers enjoy a smoother shopping experience, while brands maximize revenue from existing traffic through relevant cross-selling.

## Best Practices for Fashion Brands

To maximize the effectiveness of Complete-Look Recommendations:

- Recommend products that genuinely complement the selected item.
- Create curated outfit combinations instead of random product suggestions.
- Display recommendations on product pages and inside the cart.
- Use seasonal collections and trending styles to refresh combinations regularly.
- Monitor cross-sell click rates, attach rates, and combo revenue to optimize performance.
- Limit recommendations to three or four highly relevant products for a cleaner shopping experience.

By focusing on relevance and simplicity, fashion brands can improve both customer satisfaction and conversion rates.

For fashion retailers looking to grow revenue without increasing advertising costs, complete-look recommendations offer one of the most effective and customer-friendly strategies available.
