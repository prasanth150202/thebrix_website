Questions

Before you ask

Will Brix slow down my store?

No. Brix loads asynchronously after your page renders and weighs less than a single product image (~28 KB). It has no effect on your Core Web Vitals scores.

Does it work with my theme?

Brix works with every Online Store 2.0 theme out of the box, and matches your fonts and colors automatically. For heavily customized themes, our support team does free setup.

What exactly does Brix AI change in my store?

Only the things you’ve given it: reward tier amounts, which offers are active, and which products appear in upsells and bundles. Every change is logged, and you can undo any of them with one click.

Does it work with subscription or currency apps?

Yes. Brix supports Shopify Markets, multi-currency, and plays nicely with major subscription apps. Reward tiers convert to the shopper’s local currency automatically.

Can I try the paid features first?

Starter and Pro both start with a 14-day free trial — billed monthly through Shopify afterwards, cancel anytime. Free is free forever, no trial needed.

What happens if I uninstall?

Brix removes itself cleanly — no leftover code in your theme. Your discounts and analytics export before you go, and your store is exactly as it was.
