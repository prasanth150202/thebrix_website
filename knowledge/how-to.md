BRIX Tutorial — How to Set Up Frequently Bought Togetherx 

Customers often buy just one product because related products are not shown at the right moment. 

With BRIX Frequently Bought Together, you can recommend relevant products directly inside the cart drawer. 

From the BRIX dashboard, go to FBT in the left navigation. 

First, choose from three available templates - a compact horizontal row, card grid, or featured single product layout. 

Next, select how recommendations should be created. 

Choose Manual to select products yourself, Automatic to let BRIX recommend related products, or AI-Powered for smarter personalized suggestions on supported plans. 

Now, customize the section title, button text, price visibility, maximum products, and display limit. 

Once everything is ready, click Save. 

Finally, click Enable in theme editor. In Shopify Theme Editor, go to App embeds, turn on the FBT Widget from BRIX, and click Save. 

After enabling it, come back to BRIX and refresh the page to confirm the widget status. Once it is active, your Frequently Bought Together block will appear on your product pages. 

And that's it. 

Your Frequently Bought Together recommendations are now ready to help customers discover relevant products and increase cart value with BRIX. 

BRIX Tutorial — How to Create a Bundle Page 

Customers may want to buy multiple products together, but finding the right combination can take too much time. 

With BRIX Combo, you can create dedicated bundle pages that make product selection easier. 

From the BRIX dashboard, go to Build a Combo Pro and click Create a Bundle. 

First, choose your layout template. 

Use Guided Architect for step-by-step product selection, Velocity Stream for faster category browsing, or Editorial Split for a premium, image-focused experience. 

Next, add your steps or categories and select products directly from your Shopify catalog. 

You can also attach a discount to the bundle, customize colours, fonts, buttons, and header content. 

Need help with the content? Click the BRIX AI sparkle icon to generate titles and descriptions. 

Once your bundle page is ready, click Publish. 

BRIX automatically creates a dedicated Shopify page for your bundle. 

From the Brix dashboard, you can edit, duplicate, or manage your bundles and track views and attributed revenue. 

For deeper insights, open Brix Analytics to monitor visitors, click-through rates, and revenue from bundle orders. 

And that's it. 

Your bundle page is now ready to help customers discover better product combinations and build higher-value orders with BRIX.

Not sure what to write, recommend, or change inside BRIX?

BRIX Tutorial — How to Customize Your Cart with Cart Editor 

Want to design how your Shopify cart drawer looks and works? 

From the BRIX dashboard, go to Cart Editor. 

On the left, you will see all cart settings. On the right, you can preview your cart live. 

You can switch between Items and Empty Cart preview and turn the drawer Active or Inactive from the top bar. 

Start with Design to customize cart width, border radius, shadow, and animation. 

In General, choose when the cart should open and whether it slides from the right or left. 

In Header Style, customize the cart title, close button, colors, and border. 

Next, use the Body section to enable features like announcements, progress bar, coupon slider, upsell products, and empty cart recommendations. 

In the Footer, customize the checkout button, footer text, colors, and advanced options like custom CSS. 

Some advanced features may be available only on selected plans. 

Once everything looks good in the live preview, click Save. 

And that’s it. 

Your BRIX cart drawer is now customized and ready to create a better shopping experience. 

BRIX Tutorial — How to Set Up Coupon Banner 

Want to show promo offers directly on your Shopify product pages? 

With BRIX Coupon Banner, you can display selected discount codes near the Add to Cart button and encourage customers to buy faster. 

From the BRIX dashboard, go to Coupon Banner. 

First, turn the top-right toggle to Active. 

If the theme app embed is not enabled, BRIX will show a warning with a direct link to the Shopify Theme Editor. 

Next, choose a template: Classic Banner, Minimal Card, or Bold & Vibrant. 

Now, open Coupon Selection and choose the active Shopify discount codes you want to show. 

In Display Condition, choose where the banner should appear — on all product pages, specific products, collections, or product tags. 

Use Customize Coupon to edit the heading, subtext, colors, button style, and individual display rules for each coupon. 

In Layout, choose list, carousel, or grid style and adjust the border radius, font size, and padding. 

You can also enable a Countdown Timer to create urgency for limited-time offers. 

Finally, choose whether the banner appears above or below the Add to Cart button. 

Check the live preview, click Save, and your Coupon Banner will be ready on your product pages.
