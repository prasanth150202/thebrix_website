# Combo Forge — Bundle Builder

Starter and above to publish (locked entirely on Free). 3 bundles on Starter, unlimited on
Pro.

Combo Forge is a dedicated page builder for multi-product bundles — think "build your own
gift box" or "complete the routine" pages that live outside the standard product page. You
start by picking a layout, then configure every visual and behavioral detail without
writing code:

- **Guided Architect** — a step-by-step wizard: shoppers pick items from Collection 1, then
  Collection 2, and so on, with an optional per-step selection limit (e.g. "choose one
  cleanser, up to two serums"). Best when shoppers need to pick one item per category
  (skincare routines).
- **Velocity Stream** — a single page with switchable collection tabs, letting shoppers
  browse multiple product groups without leaving the builder. Best when items are
  interchangeable across a few collections.
- **Editorial Split** — a hero "deal of the day" card (image, price, compare-at price, CTA)
  paired with categorized product grids, and an optional rotating banner slider. Best for a
  flagship "deal of the day."
- **Simple Collection** — the lightest option: a single collection with a target bundle
  size and total product cap.

Every layout shares a common toolkit: a banner image (desktop/mobile, with fit and
full-width options), title & description styling with per-element alignment/color/weight, a
product grid with configurable columns, image ratio, hover effects, and an optional slider
mode with navigation arrows and swipe support, a progress bar that unlocks a discount once a
set number of items is added, the option to attach one of your Shopify discount codes to the
bundle, fully restylable Add-to-Cart and Checkout buttons, and an advanced Custom CSS field.

Six one-click Quick Themes (Minimal, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Royal, Noir) restyle the entire
bundle page's colors, buttons, and tabs at once — or you can set a single brand color and
apply it everywhere. Combo Forge also has an AI Suggestions toggle that recommends
products/collections to shoppers as they build their bundle.

Finished bundles are saved as reusable templates from a central dashboard, where you can
publish, unpublish, or delete them at any time.

## Combo Forge Settings Reference

The Combo Forge builder follows the same section-list pattern as the Cart Drawer:
**Layout**, **Bundle Rule / Collections**, **Title & Description**, **Banner**, **Products &
Grid**, **Styling** (product cards and, on tab layouts, tab styling), **Behavior** (preview
bar, variant selector, buttons), and **Advanced** (progress bar, coupon offer, AI
suggestions, custom CSS).

## Build Your First Combo (Bundle Page)

1. Open Combo Forge and choose "Create new."
2. Pick a layout: Guided Architect, Velocity Stream, Editorial Split, or Simple Collection.
3. Under Steps/Collections, choose which Shopify collections feed the bundle and set your
   total product cap.
4. Add a banner image, then set your title, description, and product grid styling — or
   apply a Quick Theme for a one-click restyle.
5. Optionally attach a discount code and configure the progress-bar discount unlock.
6. Save as a template, preview it, then publish it live.

## Tips

- Apply a Quick Theme first, then fine-tune individual colors — it's much faster than
  styling every element from scratch.

## Example Use Case

**Skincare — "build your routine" bundle:** A Guided Architect Combo walks shoppers through
Cleanser → Toner → Moisturizer, one collection per step, with a discount that unlocks once
3+ products are added — plus a Progress Bar in the Cart Drawer reinforcing the same
threshold.
