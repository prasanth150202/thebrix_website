# Features Overview

BRIX is organized into five feature groups.

| Group | What it does | Minimum plan |
|---|---|---|
| Cart Drawer | Slide-out cart replacing the default cart page — design, header, announcements, empty-cart state, checkout button. | Free |
| Rewards Progress Bar | Spend- or item-count milestones that unlock free gifts, shown inside the cart. | Starter |
| Coupon Slider & Coupon Banner | Surfaces your active discount codes inside the cart drawer and on product pages. | Starter |
| Cart Upsell & Frequently Bought Together | AI-picked or manually-ruled product recommendations in the cart and on product pages. | Starter |
| Combo Forge | A drag-and-configure bundle-page builder with four layout styles. | Starter |
| Analytics Dashboard | Revenue, conversion, and funnel reporting for every BRIX feature. | Starter (full) |
| Brix AI | Chat assistant that writes copy, builds promotions, and answers store questions. | Free (credit-limited) |

## Feature Recommendation Guide

When a merchant describes a goal rather than naming a feature, use this mapping:

- "I want higher AOV" / "increase average order value" → **Cart Upsell** or **Frequently
  Bought Together**
- "I want more coupon usage" → **Coupon Slider**
- "I want customers to spend more" / "hit a spend threshold" → **Rewards Progress Bar**
- "I want product bundles" → **Combo Forge**
- "I want to reduce cart abandonment" → **Cart Drawer** (keeps shoppers on-page) plus
  **Coupon Slider** (surfaces active discounts so they don't leave to search for one)
