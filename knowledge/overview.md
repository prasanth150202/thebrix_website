# App Overview

BRIX helps merchants increase average order value and conversion by making the cart itself
persuasive — with rewards, recommendations, bundles, and coupons — without writing code.
Every feature is configured through Polaris-style settings panels with a live preview, so
changes can be previewed before you publish them to your storefront, and nothing goes live
by accident.

**In one sentence:** BRIX helps merchants increase average order value and conversion by
making the cart itself persuasive — with rewards, recommendations, bundles, and coupons —
without writing code.

## What Problem the App Solves

Most Shopify themes ship with a bare-bones cart: a list of line items and a checkout button.
That's a missed opportunity — the cart is the moment a shopper has already decided to buy,
which makes it the best place on your entire site to:

- **Increase order value** — by showing shoppers how close they are to a free-shipping or
  free-gift threshold, and suggesting one more relevant product before they check out.
- **Reduce cart abandonment** — by keeping shoppers on the page (in a drawer) instead of
  navigating them away to a separate cart page, and by surfacing active coupons so they
  don't leave to search for a discount code.
- **Sell bundles, not just single items** — many products sell better as a set (a skincare
  routine, a "build your own" snack box, a starter kit). Standard Shopify product pages
  aren't built for that.
- **Save merchant time** — writing bundle copy, setting up upsell rules, and configuring
  promotions manually is slow. BRIX's AI assistant does the first draft for you.

BRIX addresses all four without requiring a developer, theme edits, or custom code.

## Getting Started (first six steps)

1. **Enable the app embed.** Open your Shopify theme editor, go to App Embeds, and turn on
   BRIX. This is required before any BRIX feature can appear on your storefront.
2. **Customize your Cart Drawer.** Open Cart Drawer in the BRIX admin and set your colors,
   layout, header, and checkout button using the live preview.
3. **Set up Frequently Bought Together.** Add product pairings (AI or manual) to start
   increasing average order value on product pages.
4. **Create a coupon banner.** Build a discount promotion and choose where it's targeted.
5. **Build your first Combo.** Pick a layout in Combo Forge and publish your first bundle
   page.
6. **Review your analytics.** Check revenue, funnel, and conversion numbers as shoppers
   start interacting with your new cart.
