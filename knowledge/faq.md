# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to edit my theme code to use BRIX?**
A: No. Every feature is configured through settings panels with a live preview. The only
manual step is turning on the BRIX app embed once, in your theme editor.

**Q: Will BRIX slow down my store?**
A: The Cart Drawer and widgets are designed to load only what's needed for the storefront
experience and don't require replacing your theme.

**Q: Can I use BRIX with any Shopify theme?**
A: BRIX works through the Shopify app embed system, which is supported by all Online Store
2.0 themes. Legacy (pre-2.0) themes are not supported.

**Q: What happens to a feature I configure on the Free plan but haven't paid to unlock?**
A: You can fully design and save it — the settings and live preview inside BRIX work
normally — but it won't be shown to shoppers on your live storefront until you upgrade to a
plan that includes it.

**Q: Does the Coupon Slider create discount codes for me?**
A: No — it displays discount codes you've already created in Shopify. Brix AI can create
the discount code for you if you ask it to.

**Q: Is the AI's product recommendation the same everywhere?**
A: No. Cart Upsell recommends based on what's currently in the cart; FBT recommends based
on the product page being viewed; Combo Forge's AI suggestions help shoppers complete a
bundle. Each can run in AI mode or manual-rules mode independently.

**Q: Can I remove the "Powered by BRIX" watermark?**
A: Yes, on the Starter and Pro plans. It's always shown on the Free plan.

**Q: How many bundle pages can I build?**
A: Zero on Free (locked), three on Starter, and unlimited on Pro.

**Q: What are "AI BRIX credits"?**
A: Each plan includes a monthly allowance of Brix AI actions (writing copy, creating a
rule, answering a question, etc.) — 10 on Free, 30 on Starter, 90 on Pro. Extra usage is
billed per credit rather than blocked.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: The Free plan itself has no time limit — it's free indefinitely, with the limitations
described in the pricing documentation. Starter and Pro both start with a 14-day free
trial.

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade at any time from the Billing dashboard.

**Q: Can I have more than one Cart Drawer design?**
A: No — one drawer configuration applies storefront-wide. Combo Forge bundle pages are
separate and can each have their own design.

**Q: Do reward products cost me anything extra?**
A: No — BRIX doesn't charge per reward given; you control which products are offered and at
what threshold.

**Q: Can shoppers stack multiple coupons?**
A: Coupon stacking follows your Shopify discount settings — BRIX displays and applies codes
but doesn't change Shopify's discount-combination rules.

**Q: Does the AI ever act without my approval?**
A: No — Brix AI always asks for confirmation before creating or changing anything in your
store.

**Q: What currency are prices shown in?**
A: The editor reflects your store's configured currency; documentation examples use ₹ and
$ interchangeably for illustration.

**Q: Can I export my analytics?**
A: Analytics are viewable in-dashboard with date-range filtering and comparison views.
