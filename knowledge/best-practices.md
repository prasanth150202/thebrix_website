# Best Practices & Tips

## Best Practices

- **Set realistic progress-bar tiers.** Base your first tier on your current average order
  value so it's achievable, and your final tier slightly above it to lift AOV without
  feeling out of reach.
- **Keep upsell rules relevant.** Manual rules that pair genuinely complementary products
  convert better than broad, unrelated suggestions — use AI mode if you're not sure what
  pairs well yet.
- **Match your bundle layout to the shopping pattern.** Use Guided Architect when shoppers
  need to pick one item per category (skincare routines); use Velocity Stream when items are
  interchangeable across a few collections; use Editorial Split for a flagship "deal of the
  day."
- **Don't stack too many cart elements.** Enabling the progress bar, coupon slider, and
  upsell products all at once can crowd the drawer — prioritize the one or two most
  relevant to your store first.
- **Review analytics weekly.** Use the funnel view to see where shoppers drop off between
  cart creation and checkout, and adjust upsell/coupon placement accordingly.
- **Let Brix AI draft, then edit.** AI-written copy is a strong starting point — always
  review it against your brand voice before publishing.

## Tips

- Use the confetti celebration sparingly — it's most effective on the final, highest-value
  tier rather than every tier.
- A countdown timer on a coupon works best when the expiry is genuinely short (hours, not
  weeks) — it loses urgency otherwise.
- Apply a Quick Theme first in Combo Forge, then fine-tune individual colors — it's much
  faster than styling every element from scratch.
- Test both the "Classic" and "Quick Add" interaction styles on Frequently Bought Together —
  Quick Add tends to convert better for lower-consideration items.
- Check the mobile preview separately from desktop — drawer width and swipe-checkout
  behavior can look different on small screens.
- Keep announcement bar text short — it's a single line and long text will wrap awkwardly
  on mobile.

## Example Use Cases

- **Skincare** — "Build your routine" bundle: a Guided Architect Combo walks shoppers
  through Cleanser → Toner → Moisturizer, one collection per step, with a discount that
  unlocks once 3+ products are added, plus a Progress Bar reinforcing the same threshold.
- **Food & beverage** — Free-shipping momentum: a two-tier Progress Bar where Tier 1
  unlocks a free sample pouch and Tier 2 unlocks free shipping, shown at the top of the Cart
  Drawer.
- **Apparel** — Complete-the-look upsell: manual Cart Upsell rules pair products with
  matching accessories, shown in the cart drawer as the shopper adds items.
